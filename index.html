The Challenge

Anymob.pk, a growing e-commerce mobile app in Pakistan, was facing intense competition from larger platforms. They needed to increase their user base while ensuring customer retention and higher average order values.

The company had limited marketing budget compared to competitors but wanted to create a distinctive value proposition that would attract and retain customers in a crowded marketplace.

The Strategy

Tiered Cashback Program

Designed a multi-level cashback system that rewarded both new users and repeat customers with increasing benefits based on purchase frequency and value.

Referral Campaign

Implemented a "Refer & Earn" program where existing users received additional cashback for each new customer they brought to the platform.

Push Notification Strategy

Created a segmented push notification system that delivered personalized cashback offers based on user browsing and purchase history.

Influencer Partnerships

Collaborated with micro-influencers in Pakistan to promote the cashback program through authentic content and exclusive promo codes.

The cashback campaign strategy delivered significant results for Anymob.pk:

  • 45% increase in new user acquisition within the first 3 months
